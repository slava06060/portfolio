About Me

I am a developing artist who enjoys expressing ideas and emotions through drawing. My primary interests include portraiture, observational drawing, still life, and studies inspired by nature.

Drawing has taught me patience, observation, and problem-solving. I am constantly experimenting with new techniques and challenging myself to improve my understanding of composition, anatomy, perspective, and visual communication. Each artwork is an opportunity to learn something new and push my abilities further.

My goal is to continue growing as an artist through formal education, creative exploration, and dedicated practice. I am currently developing both my technical skills and artistic voice while preparing for a future in the creative field.
